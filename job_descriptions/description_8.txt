We will never ask for the exchange of money or credit card details during the recruitment process. Please be aware of any suspicious email activity from people who could be pretending to be recruiters or senior individuals at Beekeeper. If in doubt, please ignore the message.IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR CANDIDATES: At Beekeeper, our team’s safety always comes first, and we equally care for the safety of our candidates. Recruitment fraud is a potential threat to job seekers, so please be aware that throughout our recruitment process, whether the recruitment is made directly by us or through a third-party agency, you’ll never be required to pay any fees or disclose personal financial details. We strongly urge that you do not disclose personal or financial details to anyone you do not know and trust.

Please be aware of any suspicious email activity from people who could be pretending to be recruiters or senior individuals at Beekeeper.
